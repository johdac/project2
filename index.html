Questions

Everything you need to know about BookShare Connect.
How do I list books?
Take photos of your books, add the title and author, and set your asking price or trade preference. Your listing goes live immediately and appears to readers in your area.
Is BookShare Connect really free?
Yes. Basic membership is free forever. You get ten listings and can trade with neighbors. Premium membership unlocks unlimited listings and regional trading for a small monthly fee.
How do trades actually work?
Message the other reader through the app to arrange pickup. Meet in a public place, exchange books, and rate each other. It's that simple. No shipping, no middleman.
What if someone doesn't show up?
Our community rating system helps keep things honest. If someone cancels or doesn't show, you can report it. Repeat offenders lose trading privileges.
Can I sell books instead of trading?
Yes. You can list books for trade, for sale, or both. Set your own prices and decide what you want in return. The choice is yours.
